16 Psyche's Location in our Solar System

Where is Psyche?

Psyche is located in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.

It is 2.5 to 3.3 times further from the sun than Earth.

Because Psyche and Earth orbit at different speeds and paths, the distance from Earth to Psyche varies from less than 186 million miles to more than 372 million miles.

To help your team understand Psyche's relative position in our Solar System, below is a reference to each planets distance from our Sun.

Understanding Measurements

Since planets are millions of kilometers away from the Sun, the unit of measurement named the Astronomical Unit (au) was created.

An au is exactly 149,597,870,700 meters / 92,955,807 miles, the approximate average distance between the Earth and the Sun, defined by the International Astronomical Union (IAU).
