A Year on Psyche

Imagine taking a super long space journey that lasts 5 whole Earth years, that's what one year is like on Psyche! This amazing asteroid zooms through space between Mars and Jupiter, taking the scenic route around the Sun. As it travels, Psyche puts on quite a show, its shiny metallic surface sparkles and glimmers like a disco ball in space, creating beautiful light shows along the way. And just like a space explorer collecting souvenirs, Psyche meets lots of other interesting space rocks and cosmic debris during its journey. Each trip around the Sun is like a new adventure, full of spectacular sights and exciting encounters with other objects in the asteroid belt. It's like the longest, most incredible space road trip ever!

A Year on Psyche

One year on the asteroid Psyche lasts about 5 Earth years as it orbits the Sun between Mars and Jupiter. During this time, Psyche experiences varying levels of solar radiation and temperatures as it travels through space. The mission will help us better understand the asteroid's orbital dynamics and its unique metallic composition.
